title: "Missing whitespace after ',', ';', or ':' (E231)"
links:
  - https://www.python.org/dev/peps/pep-0008/#pet-peeves
---

There should be whitespace after the characters `,`, `;`, and `:`.

### Anti-pattern

```python
my_tuple = 1,2,3
```

### Best practice

```python
my_tuple = 1, 2, 3
```
