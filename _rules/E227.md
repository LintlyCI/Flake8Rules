title: "Missing whitespace around bitwise or shift operator (E227)"
links:
  - https://www.python.org/dev/peps/pep-0008/#pet-peeves
---

There should be one space before and after bitwise and shift operators (`<<`, `>>`, `&`, `|`, `^`).

### Anti-pattern

```python
x = 128<<1
```

### Best practice

```python
x = 128 << 1
```
